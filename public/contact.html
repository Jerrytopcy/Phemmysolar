Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations are completed within 1-3 days, while commercial projects may take 1-2 weeks depending on system size.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We offer flexible financing plans with low interest rates to make solar affordable for everyone.

What warranty do you provide?

We provide a 10-year comprehensive warranty on installations and 25-year warranty on panels.

Will solar work during rainy seasons?

Yes! Solar panels still generate electricity on cloudy days, though at reduced efficiency. Our systems are designed for Nigeria's climate.

What if my roof needs repair?

We can remove and reinstall your system if roof maintenance is needed, free of charge within the warranty period.

How much can I save on electricity?

Most customers save 70-90% on their electricity bills depending on system size and usage patterns.
