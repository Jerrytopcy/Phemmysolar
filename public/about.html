About PhemmySolar Nigeria

Your trusted partner in renewable energy solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2015, PhemmySolar Nigeria has been at the forefront of the renewable energy revolution in Nigeria. What started as a small team of passionate solar enthusiasts has grown into a leading provider of premium solar solutions serving thousands of satisfied customers across the nation.

Our mission is simple: to make clean, affordable solar energy accessible to every home and business in Nigeria. We believe in the power of the sun to transform lives and communities.
